BLOOD DETECTOR

Currently, many rural areas of Peru still do not have a specialized medical center to perform different blood tests. Many of the equipment necessary for this purpose require a high economic investment and qualified personnel. This work proposes a fast and inexpensive system for the recognition of 3 types of blood cells based on convolutional networks.
